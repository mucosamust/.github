## 🚀 How to Get Involved

Whether you are committing your first line of code or optimizing a complex compiler, there is a place for you here.

### 1. Claim Your Seat
*   **Request Access:** Drop your GitHub username in our official community channels or open an issue in the onboarding repository to get invited to the organization.
*   **Update Your Profile:** Make your membership public on your GitHub profile so everyone knows you are part of the team!

### 2. Make Your First Contribution
*   Look for repositories tagged with `good-first-issue` or check out our `first-contributions` sandbox.
*   Submit a Pull Request (PR) to add your portfolio or project to our curated **Awesome MUST Computing** list.

### 3. Follow the Golden Rules
*   **Respect the Workflow:** Always cut a branch (`feature/your-feature`) instead of pushing directly to `main`.
*   **Write Clear PRs:** Describe your changes and explain *why* you made them.
*   **Be a Helpful Peer:** Review other students' code constructively—we are all here to learn.
